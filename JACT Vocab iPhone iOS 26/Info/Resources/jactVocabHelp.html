These are flashcards representing the vocabulary to be learned for the first 19 lessons of the excellent first-year Ancient Greek textbook Reading Greek, Second Edition, by the Joint Association of Classical Teachers (JACT), Cambridge University Press.

The cards are sorted in the order in which vocabulary is presented in the textbook. Swipe, or tap at the right or left edge of a card, to navigate to the next or previous card. You can also tap a lesson number or section letter to navigate to the first card of the next lesson or section.

Use the Key button to toggle display of the English translation. This is good for self-testing on vocabulary memorization.

Use the Folder button to select a section to jump to directly.

Use the Search button to view the entire vocabulary alphabetically.

Use the Bulb button to drill yourself on a subset of the cards in random order.

This program is based on my JACT Vocabulary HyperCard stack, whose origins go back to 1991. It was then ported to a Mac OS X-native application. The program was then rewritten to match the 2nd edition of the JACT. Now, it is also ported to iPhone. This program is free for all. Share and enjoy.
